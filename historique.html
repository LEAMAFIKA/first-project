Historique

BEJAMIN DAMELIN in 1943, Damelin college started as a humble remedial school, but it wasn't long before Damelin correspondence college and Damelin correspondence college and professional Evening School were estabished, in 1955 and 1962 respectively,providing a platform of learning for those wanting to further their education while holding down day jobs. 1966-1983, It was soon after that ,that Damelin began to expand their physical footprint with a view to going national , opening campus in Durban (1966),johannesburg (1968)and cape town (1983). what was followed,was a period of exponential growth,with more than 16 campuses being opened in more than 17 city centres around the country over the next 10 years.
